Kiwi Aelin ek paek jaen faras sere jaen gwang na

Kiwi-ism, and its branches can be traced all throughout kiwinesia, from [one billion BC] to today. Kiwi-ism is pretty cool

Universal Kiwinist Concepts:

Layers of the Kiwi
  • White center - Earth, human beings
  • Seeds - Stars, Kiwi Aelin’s guardians, bringers of life
  • Green part - Heaven, afterlife
  • Outer brown layer - Kiwi Aelin’s control over the universe
